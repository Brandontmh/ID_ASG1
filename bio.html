All about us

Ashin

Chen Hsin-hung nicknamed was born on 6 December 1975 in Beitou District, Taiwan. He was born into a family of four, consisting of his parents, himself and a younger brother. His family owned a record store, which inspired his passion for music. Ashin's childhood ambition was to become a singer, but his vocal range was too narrow. He claims his bandmates selected him to be Mayday's singer as he was the worst instrumentalist out of all of them. Ashin can play both guitar and drums. He is a Taiwanese singer-songwriter, author, and the vocalist of the Taiwanese band Mayday. Ashin majored in art in college, where he also began his musical career. He is also the founder and designer of the clothing line StayReal and a shareholder of the music label B'in Music. He was elected as a board member of the Taiwan Public Television Service in 2013.

Monster

Monster or Wen Shang-yi was born on 28 November 1976, in Hsinchu County, Taiwan. He is one of the two guitarists and leader of the Taiwanese rock band, Mayday. Monster became interested in music and the guitar while studying at the Affiliated Senior High School of National Taiwan Normal University where he also met three other Mayday members Ashin, Stone and Masa. He later became vice-president of the guitar society in high school and president of the Rock and Roll society at National Taiwan University during his university days

Stone

Shi Jinhang nicknamed Stone was born on 11 December,1975 in Taipei , Taiwan. The guitarist of Taiwan Orchestra Mayday, once changed his name to "Shi Hangwei" and later used his original name "Shi Jinhang". Shi Jinhang graduated from Class 818 of the High School Affiliated to National Normal University . He and Martha served as the vice president of the guitar club in the same term. He is also a member of the swimming team of the High School Affiliated to Normal University. Graduated from the Department of Water Environment of Tamkang University and graduated from Liverpool School of Performing Arts.

Masa

Cai Shengyan nicknamed Masa, was born on 25 April, 1977 in Kaohsiung , Taiwan. The bassist of the Taiwanese Orchestra Mayday is also one of the founders of Faith Music. Cai Shengyan graduated from Zhongzheng National Elementary School in Lingya District, Kaohsiung City, and Class 819 of the High School Affiliated to National Normal University. He and Shitou served as the president of the Guitar Club in the same term and the chairman of the Society. Graduated from the Sociology Department of Fu Jen Catholic University. In addition to the bass, other instruments include double bass, guitar, piano and harmonica.

Guan-You

Liu Guanyou was born on 28 July, 1973 in Miaoli , Taiwan. The drummer of the Taiwanese Orchestra Mayday is also one of the founders of Belief Music and the founder of the trendy clothing brand "Drumming Fascinated". He graduated from Mingren Middle School and Guoguang Art School. Good at jazz drums and jazz piano. Mayday's fourth drummer, who joined in 1999, is the oldest and latest member of the band. Before joining Mayday, he was the drummer of the independent band "Why Not", and released the album "Unstoppable" in 1995; the group was famous in the independent music circle at that time for its unique and fresh jazz music style.
